# Implementation Plan - Performance Matrix QOL & Interactivity

This plan focuses on making the Performance Matrix more interactive and building out the educational infrastructure for restaurant managers.

## Phase 1: Visual Polish (Performance Matrix) [COMPLETED]
- **Dot Styling**: Removed thick white outlines for cleaner visuals.
- **Hover Glow**: Subtle interactive scaling on dots.

## Phase 2: Category Filtering (Legend Interactivity) [COMPLETED]
- **Toggle Visibility**: Quadrant cards are now interactive filters.
- **Global Sync**: Filtering updates chart and table simultaneously.

## Phase 3: Zoom and Navigation [COMPLETED]
- **Navigation Controls**: Zoom In/Out/Reset buttons added to chart.
- **Dynamic Domains**: State-driven axis scaling based on zoom level.

## Phase 4: Educational "How it Works" Infrastructure [NEW]
- **Step-by-Step Modal**: Implement a premium glassmorphism modal triggered by the nav link.
    - **Step 1: Data Ingestion**: Explaining the "Clean & Mapping" process simply.
    - **Step 2: Financial Engineering**: Explaining Profit Margin vs. Popularity calculation.
    - **Step 3: Strategic Action**: Explaining how the AI generates unique insights.
- **Visual Storytelling**: Use icons and simple typography to bridge the gap between "Raw Data" and "Executive Strategy."

## Phase 5: "Resources" & Glossary [NEW]
- **Glossary Page/Section**: A dedicated area explaining the "4 Quadrants" in deep but simple terms.
    - **Stars**: Your high-margin, high-volume champions.
    - **Plowhorses**: Popular favorites that need margin optimization.
    - **Puzzles**: Profitable secrets that need better marketing.
    - **Dogs**: Underperformers that need rebranding or removal.
- **Manager Toolkit**: Links to best practices in menu design and psychological pricing.

## Phase 6: Final Transitions & UX
- **Navigation Link Fixes**: Update nav links to correctly anchor or trigger modals.
- **Cross-Component Highlighting**: Pulse effects between table and chart hovers.
